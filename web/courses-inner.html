A Scientific program and hands-on training for Autologous Fat Grafting to the face. All courses are taught by experienced Aesthetic physicians and surgeons. The students will learn advanced harvesting and grafting applications & techniques. By attending this program, doctors should be able to consult and select patients for fat grafting. Training will cover indications, contraindications, and proper preoperative preparation. The course reviews multiple advanced techniques for facial augmentation. Course manual and all materials are included in course fees.

  • Discuss history and current advances in facial fat grafting
  • Identify relevant surgical anatomy for areas of treatment
  • Conduct efficient consultation and patient selection
  • Identify indications, contraindications, and proper preoperative preparation
  • Learn Advanced Harvesting and Grafting Applications & Techniques
  • Learn the necessary staff requirements, supplies and equipment
  • Discuss the improvement of fat grafting by platelet rich plasma
  • Discuss postoperative care
  • Identify possible complications of Fat Grafting, management, and how to avoid them
  • Discuss PRP and Stem cell techniques and the advantages of adipose-derived regenerative cells
  • Understand the unique demands of the Aesthetic patients in 2015, in a highly competitive market, and how to distinguish yourself from the competition
